Tactics and Strategies

Learn the best strategies that top players use to win games! Here, we break down powerful team setups, hero pairings, and winning plans for different situations. Find tips on how to counter popular heroes, work well with your team, and stay flexible in any match.

Popular Hero Combos

  • Tank & Support: For a tough defense, pair Aegis with Revitalize to keep your frontline strong.
  • Assassin & Support: Sneakily target enemies in the back. Shade and Hex work well to surprise and take down their support.
  • Tank & Marksman: Pair Atlas with Bruno for devastating crowd control and burst damage. Atlas’s Fatal Links skill combined with Bruno’s Volley Shot makes for a deadly follow-up.
  • Mage & Fighter: Use Lylia with Thamuz to handle sustained fights. Lylia’s area damage and Thamuz’s life steal allow them to maintain a strong presence in team fights.
  • Fighter & Assassin: Pair Chou with Ling for high mobility and single-target takedowns. Chou’s The Way of the Dragon with Ling’s burst damage can eliminate key targets quickly.
  • Tank & Mage: Combine Tigreal with Pharsa for massive area damage. Tigreal’s Implosion can group enemies, allowing Pharsa to rain down her ultimate for huge AoE damage.

Beating Common Strategies

Facing an aggressive enemy? Try using a defensive setup to wear them down. Got an enemy targeting your support? Learn how to protect key heroes by keeping your team in good formation.

Quick Tips

  • Stay Alert: Keep an eye on where the enemy might be, and use the map to your advantage.
  • Control Key Areas: Use heroes with abilities that can keep enemies out of certain areas, so fights happen where you’re strongest.
  • Be Caution: We recommend to ban Chou and Fanny if one of your team member is Biemar
